\section{Version-specific arXiv citations}

Papers with specific versions: \cite{2112.00006v1,2301.12345v2,1907.12345v3}.

ArXiv citations with explicit prefix: \cite{arXiv:2112.00006,arXiv:hep-th/9803103,arXiv:2301.12345v1}.

\section{Malformed citations}

Empty citations should be ignored: \cite{}.

Very long InspireHEP ID: \cite{123456789012345}.

Suspicious formats: \cite{not-a-valid-format,123abc456,random_string_2023}.

Mixed valid and invalid: \cite{1724847,,2112.00006,}.

\section{Special characters and formatting}

Citations with underscores: \cite{test_paper_2023,another_test_reference}.

Citations with dashes: \cite{hep-th/0501001,some-paper-2022}.

Multiple spaces and formatting issues: \cite{ 1724847 , 2112.00006 , spaced_key }.

\section{Complex citation patterns}

Nested or complex LaTeX: The result in Eq.~\eqref{eq:main} follows from \cite{1724847} and builds on earlier work \cite{hep-th/9803103,unknown_complicated_key_with_numbers_123_xyz}.

\begin{equation}
\label{eq:main}
E = mc^2 \cite{1724847}
\end{equation}
